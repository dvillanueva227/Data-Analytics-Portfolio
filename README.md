🖥️ Global Electronics Retailer — Revenue & Profitability Dashboard

Tool: Excel | Type: Sales Analytics | Status: Complete

An independent analysis of a global electronics retailer operating across 9 markets. Built a fully interactive Excel dashboard using multi-table data modeling, XLOOKUP for currency normalization, and connected slicers filtering 6 pivot charts simultaneously.

Key Findings:


United States drives 39% of total revenue at $23.8M — nearly double the next closest market
Computers generate both the highest revenue ($21.1M) and highest profit ($12.3M) of any category
Online sales at $12.4M outperform every physical country market except the US — a major growth signal
Overall profit margin sits at 59%, driven by Computers and Home Appliances


Skills demonstrated: XLOOKUP, multi-table star schema modeling, currency normalization, pivot tables, interactive slicers, dashboard design
